Running Out Of Time

Par: Dav-G-F

A breaking branch,
A rippling leaf,
The beast that followed me,

I ran, and ran, not looking back,
Until I could not breathe,

I ran so long,
My mind now gone,
I never realized,

The beast was me,
And what I sought,
Was to catch up to time.
